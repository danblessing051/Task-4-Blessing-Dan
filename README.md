# 📊 Project 4: Data Visualization & Storytelling

## Overview

This project focuses on transforming raw business data into meaningful visual insights through data visualization and storytelling techniques.

Using Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn, I analyzed an e-commerce dataset containing 1,200 customer orders spanning January 2023 to June 2025. The objective was to identify key business trends, communicate findings effectively, and provide actionable recommendations based on the data.

This project is part of my Data Analytics Internship with DecodeLabs.

---

## Project Objectives

* Visualize business performance metrics
* Identify revenue trends and sales patterns
* Analyze customer acquisition channels
* Evaluate order fulfillment performance
* Present findings through clear and compelling visualizations
* Translate data insights into business recommendations

## Visualizations Created

### 1. Order Status Distribution

Purpose:

* Analyze completed, cancelled, and returned orders.

Key Insight:

* Approximately 41.4% of all orders were cancelled or returned, highlighting a significant operational challenge.

---

### 2. Revenue by Product

Purpose:

* Identify products contributing the most revenue.

Key Insight:

* Chairs generated the highest total revenue among all product categories.

---

### 3. Referral Source Performance

Purpose:

* Evaluate customer acquisition channels.

Key Insight:

* Instagram produced the highest number of orders, making it the strongest referral channel.

---

### 4. Monthly Revenue Trend

Purpose:

* Analyze revenue fluctuations over time.

Key Insight:

* Revenue varied significantly between months with no consistent upward growth trend.

---

### 5. Payment Method Distribution

Purpose:

* Understand customer payment preferences.

Key Insight:

* Certain payment methods were used more frequently, providing opportunities to optimize payment experiences.

---

### 6. Quantity Sold by Product

Purpose:

* Determine product demand and inventory priorities.

Key Insight:

* High-volume products can guide inventory management and procurement planning.

---

## Key Business Insights

* Total Revenue exceeded $1.26 million.
* Average Order Value was approximately $1,054.
* Chairs generated the highest overall revenue.
* Instagram was the leading customer acquisition source.
* 41.4% of orders were cancelled or returned.
* Revenue showed fluctuations without a sustained growth trend.

---

## Business Recommendations

### Improve Order Fulfillment

Investigate the causes of cancellations and returns to improve customer satisfaction and reduce revenue loss.

### Invest in High-Performing Marketing Channels

Increase focus on Instagram marketing campaigns due to its strong contribution to customer acquisition.

### Explore Seasonal Trends

Conduct deeper analysis into monthly revenue patterns to identify seasonal opportunities and risks.

### Strengthen Customer Retention

Implement customer loyalty and engagement strategies to drive repeat purchases and long-term growth.

### Optimize Inventory Planning

Prioritize inventory management for high-demand and high-revenue product categories.
